Donghao Zhu

Assistant Professor

Technical University of Munich

Donghao Zhu is a Professor of Engineering Systems at TUM. He is considered one of the premier thought leaders in supply chain management and business analytics. His research, see Data Science Lab, focuses on developing and implementing robust and efficient techniques for operations management. He has published widely in professional journals on both practical and theoretical aspects of supply chain and revenue management.
